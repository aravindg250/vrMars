Use Live Virtual Reality To Experience Mars With Actual Astronauts

What is VR Mars?

  • VR Mars is an event where the people of Earth can experience Mars through live virtual reality.
  • This immersive experience enables the user to witness the surface of Mars as well as some of its notable landmarks.
  • Our astronaut crew will give you a glimpse into their daily routines and show you around the base. You can experience the red planet from the comfort of your home!

Frequently Asked Questions

To sign up for VR Mars, check out our website Sign Up page, where you will be asked for your email and name. On the day of the event you will be sent a link to the VR tour.

Anyone who is curious about Mars can attend this virtual tour. Users can connect their phones to the VR headsets and simply experience the tour through screens. For optimal viewing pleasure an oculus is recommended but VR Mars is compatible with both smartphones and tablets.

The tour is hosted by Expedition Mars. On your tour you will be accompanied by one of the members of our experienced astronaut crew who happen to be the first people on Mars. To read more about our crew, check out our Meet The Astronauts page.

Thanks to new and upcoming technology our tour can be experienced in the comfort of your home. Our mission is to provide everyone with an experience of Mars to further educate the public on our solar system.

VR Mars is free thanks to our sponsors and donations. We encourage you to donate so that we can continue our journey of educating the public about Mars.

Each tour is 2 hours in length. However, users will also get an additional 1 hour to experience Mars on their own. Tours must be scheduled a week in advance and can be scheduled from any time of the day.

Each tour will start at 3:00 p.m. EST

Our virtual tours run from November 20, 2030 to November 24, 2030. Tours must be scheduled at least a week in advance and can be scheduled from any time of the day.

Users can connect their phones to the VR headsets and simply experience the tour through screens. For optimal viewing pleasure, an oculus is recommended. In addition, VR mars is compatible with both computers and tablets.
